LEVENINE

"The universe speaks in frequencies—I just translate."

Music was never just a hobby—it was my escape. A world where I could be anyone, say anything, and feel everything. I started producing music when I had no one, no distractions, no escape from reality—so I created my own. Inspired by the legends like KSHMR, ILLENIUM, Martin Garrix, and above all, KYGO, I found a way to channel emotions into sound. My dream? To capture the essence of joy and melancholy in a single beat, just like Kygo does. Because music isn’t just heard, it’s felt.

Genres & Styles

  2. Progressive House
  3. Other Experimental Sounds

Achievements & Projects

My remix of "Oha Moe Bce" by NACHAEV has reached almost 100k plays.

Inspiration & Philosophy

Kygo’s ability to merge happy and sad emotions within a single track inspires me. That’s the genre I’ll never get tired of.
